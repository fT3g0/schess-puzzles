# Roadmap

## Puzzle quality and curation

- Add local human review labels such as accepted, too easy, trivial, engine dubious, interesting, hard, and beautiful.
- Store a separate quality score without deleting filtered puzzles. Candidate factors: line length, H/E involvement, delayed material gain, eval gap, depth confirmation, and non-trivial forcing replies.
- Build a review queue that prioritizes unreviewed, suspicious, and high-potential puzzles.
- Add duplicate and motif clustering so one repeated tactic type does not dominate the public set.
- Keep the suggested-FEN path as a quality-mining route for manually spotted positions.
- Treat self-play as experimental motif generation until settings produce more non-obvious forcing lines.
